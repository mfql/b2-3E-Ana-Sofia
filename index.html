Introducing Zeolite Purificador

Contaminated water from the seas, rivers or lakes is one of the world's problems. On average, 600 liters of water are contaminated per day. Our proposals is a water filter with zeolite stone since this stone absorbs contaminants from water such as heavy metals.

Zeolite is a group of minerals used as a moisture absorber and pollutant detector. We will use the zeolite as a water cleaner since if we consume contaminated water or with heavy metals, it damages our lungs and brain and can interfere with the production of red blood cells that carry oxygen to all parts of the body.

Materials

Zeolite, cotton, fine sand, rocks and a recycle bottle
